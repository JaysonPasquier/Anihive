How to use streaming episode players

1. Select language

When you arrive at the episode page, the first step is to select the language in which you want to watch the episode. You can choose from the options available in the drop-down menu or buttons.

2. Choose the season

Next, choose the season of the anime you want to watch. You will find a list of available seasons just below the language selector.

3. Select episode

After selecting the language and season, choose the specific episode you want to watch. Episodes are listed in numerical order for easy navigation.

4. Use the player

Once the episode is selected, the player will load automatically. You can use the player controls to play, pause, fast forward, or rewind the episode. Make sure you have a stable internet connection for a smooth viewing experience.

5. Different episode players

We tried to find the best player, with as few ads as possible, the vostfr and vf players normally have no ads and are the best, For the other languages ​​we will do the best possible to have as few ads as possible.

6. Enjoy your episode

Sit back and enjoy your episode! Don't forget to share your opinions and comments with the AniHive community.
